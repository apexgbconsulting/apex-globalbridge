About Apex GlobalBridge Consulting

We Are Your Strategic Bridge to Global Success

Apex GlobalBridge Consulting was founded with a singular mission: to eliminate the language barriers that prevent Latin America's most talented professionals from reaching their full potential in the global marketplace.

We combine world-class Business English instruction, executive communication coaching, and cutting-edge AI technology to deliver learning experiences that are personalized, practical, and measurable. Our programs are designed to help professionals communicate with confidence, lead international teams, excel in high-stakes meetings, and unlock new career opportunities.

Our team of certified language trainers, business professionals, and technology specialists works together to create transformational learning experiences that bridge the gap between local talent and global success.

At Apex GlobalBridge Consulting, we don't simply teach English — we develop the communication skills, executive presence, and cross-cultural competence required to thrive in today's international business environment.

As our clients progress and become qualified for international opportunities, we also offer career mobility support services, including professional resume optimization, interview preparation, international job search guidance, and introductions to our network of international executive recruiters and headhunters. For clients pursuing opportunities abroad, we provide visa application preparation support and document organization assistance, helping them navigate the administrative requirements associated with global career advancement. While we do not provide legal advice or immigration representation, we work alongside qualified professionals when specialized legal services are required.

Meet Our Founder

Bridging Language, Business & Opportunity

Perry Ieropoli is a graduate of the University of Miami with more than 15 years of real-world business experience working with professionals across diverse industries and international markets. Combining a strong business background with a passion for education and professional development, Perry specializes in helping individuals improve their English communication skills, prepare for interviews, advance their careers, and confidently navigate opportunities in today's global marketplace.

As a multilingual professional and TEFL-certified English instructor, Perry understands the challenges that non-native English speakers face when pursuing international business opportunities, higher education, and career advancement. His practical approach focuses on real-world communication, business vocabulary, interview preparation, professional networking, and workplace success.

Throughout his career, Perry has worked with professionals from technology, telecommunications, project management, sales, business development, logistics, and customer-facing industries — bridging the gap between academic English and the practical communication skills required in today's competitive global economy. Prior to founding Apex GlobalBridge, Perry served as a Project Manager in Clinical Research, managing complex, high-stakes projects across international teams and regulatory environments — an experience that sharpened his understanding of precision communication in global professional settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Intake & Client Assessment fee?
Intake and Client Assessments are $350,000 COP, which will be deducted from the client's package purchase upon enrollment.
Can installment payments be made for each program purchase?
Yes, installments can be made monthly for each purchased program.
Do you accept payments in cryptocurrency?
Yes, we accept payments in BTC and ETH.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at the start of any billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference.
Are sessions recorded for later review?
All live sessions are recorded and available for 90 days in your learning dashboard. AI coaching sessions are saved indefinitely.
What level of English do I need to start?
From Intermediate to Executive-Level Fluency. We serve professionals from B1 to C2 English proficiency levels. Every client starts with a 15-minute onboarding and AI-powered assessment that evaluates speaking, vocabulary, grammar, and professional communication skills. Based on the results, we develop a personalized strategic learning roadmap focused on achieving real-world outcomes — whether that's leading meetings, delivering presentations, negotiating with confidence, or advancing your international career.
